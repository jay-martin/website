Highlights

Marriage penalties occur when the EITC a married couple receives is less than the sum of the EITC's each would receive if they were single

Some very low-income couples incur marriage bonuses. Specifically, a marriage bonus can occur if:
(1) both partners are in or just over the phase-in region of their respective individual EITC's, and
(2) they have at least one but not more than three children.
